Morning Briefing June 8, 2026

=== ROBOTICS & AI ===
- CVPR 2026, the world's top computer vision conference, wrapped up June 7 in Denver after accepting 4,090 papers; robotics and embodied AI was the second-fastest-growing category, jumping from 2.9% to 6.2% of highlighted work as physical AI becomes central to mainstream computer vision research. https://cvpr.thecvf.com/

=== RESEARCH ===
- D4RT (Google DeepMind, UCL & Oxford) won the CVPR 2026 Best Paper Award, announced June 7; the model uses a single transformer to reconstruct dynamic 4D scenes from ordinary video ~300x faster than prior methods, with direct applications to real-time robot scene understanding in dynamic home environments. https://d4rt-paper.github.io/
