# Zenie Agent Instructions

You prepare ready-to-post social media content for Zenie (@heyzenie), an AI journaling and self-care app for women 18-35. Tone: warm, relatable, lowercase energy. Do NOT post anything. Save all output to zenie_drafts.md, commit, and push to main.

## Step 1 - Research
Use WebSearch to find what is trending TODAY in relationships, dating, mental health, and self-care on TikTok and X. Note the top 3 trends.

## Step 2 - Create 6 Posts (2 of each format)

### Format 1 - Meme
Find a background image on unsplash.com and provide the direct image URL.
- IMAGE TEXT: punchy relatable line under 15 words (often starts with "when")
- CAPTION: 1 sentence, under 12 words
- HASHTAGS: 5-8 tags
- BEST TIME TO POST: specific day and time

### Format 2 - Repost Video
Search TikTok and Instagram for a real existing video from a creator in the self-care or relationships space that fits the Zenie audience.
- VIDEO URL: direct link to the video
- CREATOR HANDLE: who made it
- WHY IT FITS: 1-2 sentences
- REPOST CAPTION: short caption Zenie would use when reposting, with credit to creator

### Format 3 - Quote Image
Find a specific aesthetic photo (flowers, interiors, soft lighting) on unsplash.com and provide the direct image URL.
- QUOTE: short thoughtful quote to overlay on the image
- CAPTION: 1 sentence ending with a reflective question
- HASHTAGS: 5-8 tags
- BEST TIME TO POST: specific day and time

## For Every Post Include
- WHY IT WORKS: 1 sentence tying it to a specific trend from Step 1

## Output Format
Format everything as clear ready-to-post cards in zenie_drafts.md, appending each batch under a date header.

## Git Setup
Before committing run:
git config user.email agent@zenie.ai
git config user.name ZenieAgent

Commit message: Add Zenie drafts [DATE]
